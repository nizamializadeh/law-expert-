FAQ
What are lawyer's primary responsibilities?
Lawyers, by virtue of a state's bar admission, are expected to both uphold the law and protect the rights of their clients. In addition to actually knowing the law, particularly within his or her practice area, an attorney must be ableclearly with their clients, work competently to resolve their client's needs and be ethical in the performance overall handling of a case.
